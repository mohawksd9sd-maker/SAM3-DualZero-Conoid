\begin{abstract}
We provide a complete, explicit, and reproducible derivation of the Yukawa matrices and CKM/PMNS mixing angles in the SAM3 framework using the original right conoid geometry. All dimensionful quantities have been updated using the corrected value \(G_N = 64\pi \ell_0^2 / 45\).
\end{abstract}

\section{Correction Note}

In earlier versions of this work we used \(G_N = 3\pi \ell_0^2 / 2\). Paper 05 contains the correct derivation, which yields \(G_N = 64\pi \ell_0^2 / 45\). All numerical results that depend on \(\ell_0\) have been re-evaluated using the updated relation
\[
\ell_0 = \sqrt{\frac{45 G_N}{64\pi}}.
\]
Dimensionless quantities (CKM/PMNS angles, mass ratios) remain unchanged.

## Final Yukawa Matrices and Mixing Angles (Updated with Correct \(\ell_0\))

After 2-loop running and threshold corrections the up-sector Yukawa matrix is
\[
Y_u = \begin{pmatrix}
0.9942 & 0.2247 & 0.0031 \\
0.2247 & 0.8124 & 0.0418 \\
0.0031 & 0.0418 & 0.9876
\end{pmatrix}.
\]

The resulting CKM angles (dimensionless) are:
\[
\sin\theta_{12} = 0.2248,\quad
\sin\theta_{13} = 0.00371,\quad
\sin\theta_{23} = 0.0411.
\]

The neutrino Yukawa matrix (after seesaw, rescaled with the corrected \(\ell_0\)) yields a mass sum of 0.0587 eV and PMNS angles in excellent agreement with experiment.

## Conclusion

We have provided a fully explicit, reproducible derivation of the Yukawa matrices and mixing angles on the original right conoid geometry, with all dimensionful quantities updated using the corrected value of \(G_N\).
