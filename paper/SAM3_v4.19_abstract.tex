\begin{abstract}
We present a novel non-commutative geometric framework that derives gravity and the Standard Model from the right conoid surface equipped with a custom Dual-Zero hyperreal algebra and 12-bridge icosahedral symmetry.

The model combines an infinite ruled conoid manifold with the standard finite non-commutative algebra of the non-commutative Standard Model, augmented by binary icosahedral symmetry acting as a Grand Symmetry. The Dual-Zero regularization resolves previous technical obstructions and provides a geometric origin for chirality and mass hierarchy.

We derive the Einstein–Hilbert action with an explicit formula for Newton’s constant. The information current is shown to be stationary precisely on the critical line, giving a new variational approach to the Riemann Hypothesis.
\end{abstract}
