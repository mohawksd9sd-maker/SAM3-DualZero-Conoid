\begin{abstract}
We present a geometric unification framework based on a single 2D right conoid manifold equipped with a Dual-Zero hyperreal regulator and 12 discrete bridges carrying binary-icosahedral (2I) symmetry. From this minimal structure we derive: (i) Newton's constant \(G_N = 64\pi \ell_0^2/45\), (ii) exactly three chiral fermion generations, (iii) hierarchical Yukawa matrices and realistic CKM/PMNS mixing angles from eigenmode overlaps, (iv) neutrino masses via seesaw, (v) the Higgs sector, and (vi) a consistent almost-commutative 4D lift. The framework satisfies all Connes spectral triple axioms in the Lorentzian setting (Paper 17) and passes extensive numerical robustness tests (Paper 18). Only two inputs (\(\ell_0\) anchored to \(m_t\), \(\omega_0\)) are used; all other observables are derived. We compare with existing approaches and list falsifiable predictions.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction and Motivation}
The Standard Model plus gravity remains the most successful description of nature, yet lacks a geometric origin. Noncommutative geometry (NCG) programs (Connes–Chamseddine) have shown promise but typically require significant algebraic input. SAM3 reverses this: we begin with a concrete low-dimensional geometric object — the right conoid — and let the spectrum and overlaps dictate the particle content and couplings.

\section{The Right Conoid Geometry}
The base manifold is the right conoid
\[
\mathbf{r}(u,v) = (u\cos v,\ u\sin v,\ \ell_0 \sin(2v)), \quad u\in[0,\infty),\ v\in[0,2\pi),
\]
with induced metric
\[
ds^2 = du^2 + (u^2 + 4\ell_0^2 \cos^2(2v))\,dv^2.
\]
Negative curvature patches naturally suppress the cosmological term. Twelve discrete bridges at icosahedral positions carry the 2I representation, producing exactly three chiral generations (Paper 07).

\section{The Dual-Zero Spectral Triple}
The almost-commutative spectral triple \((\mathcal{A},\mathcal{H},D)\) uses the regularized Dirac operator
\[
D_\varepsilon = D \cdot \mathrm{Reg}_2(\varepsilon),\qquad \varepsilon(n)=\omega_0 (-1)^n n^{-n}
\]
via ultrapower construction (Paper 02). Full Lorentzian axiom verification, self-adjointness, Krein structure, and absence of fermion doubling are proven in Paper 17.

\section{Key Derivations}
- **Gravity**: Seeley–DeWitt \(a_4\) coefficient on the 4D lift yields \(G_N = 64\pi \ell_0^2/45\) exactly (Paper 05).
- **Flavor**: Yukawa matrices from overlaps of regularized 2D Dirac eigenmodes with 2I projectors (Paper 04). Hierarchical structure emerges geometrically.
- **Neutrino & Higgs**: Type-I seesaw and quartic potential from the same overlaps (Paper 06).
- **Dimensional Lift**: Almost-commutative product construction (Paper 09–10).

\section{Comparison to Alternatives and Predictivity}
SAM3 uses only two inputs versus 12–18 in random-matrix or traditional NCG models. Flavor hierarchies are geometric rather than imposed (see Paper 19 for full comparison table).

Falsifiable predictions (Paper 19):
- Precise neutrino mass sum testable by KATRIN/cosmology.
- Higgs self-coupling at HL-LHC/FCC.
- No new particles below ~10 TeV; right-handed neutrinos at seesaw scale.
- Modified gravity signatures above \(10^{18}\) GeV.
