\begin{abstract}
We present a comprehensive summary of the SAM3 framework, which derives the full Standard Model fermion content, gauge and Higgs sectors, and Einstein gravity from a single geometric object — the infinite right conoid — equipped with a Dual-Zero hyperreal spectral triple and 12-bridge icosahedral symmetry. All results use the corrected Newton constant \(G_N = 64\pi \ell_0^2 / 45\). The model contains only two fundamental parameters (\(\ell_0\) and \(\omega_0\)) and makes several sharp, testable predictions.
\end{abstract}
