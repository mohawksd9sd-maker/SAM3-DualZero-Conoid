\begin{abstract}
SAM3 v4.21 is a noncommutative geometric framework that unifies classical gravity and the full Standard Model from a single geometric object: the infinite right conoid manifold equipped with the Dual-Zero hyperreal regulator and 12-bridge binary-icosahedral (\(2I\)) symmetry.

The spectral action on this structure derives the Einstein--Hilbert term with the explicit formula \(G_N = 64\pi \ell_0^2 / 45\) and recovers the Standard Model gauge group, chiral fermions, and \emph{exactly three generations}. A new analytic derivation shows that the hierarchical Yukawa, CKM, and PMNS matrices emerge directly from 2I-projected eigenmode overlaps on the conoid, with closed-form expressions controlled solely by \(\ell_0\) and \(\omega_0 \approx 0.97\). A variational principle on the information current enforces stationarity on the critical line \(\operatorname{Re}(s) = 1/2\).

All claims are supported by rigorous Lorentzian spectral triple axioms (Paper 17), full numerical convergence (Paper 18), and explicit overlap integrals. The framework is fully reproducible via the public repository.
\end{abstract}

\[

\]
with the induced metric
\[
ds^2 = du^2 + \bigl(u^2 + 4\ell_0^2 \cos^2(2v)\bigr) \, dv^2.

The 12 discrete rulings at angles \(v_k = 2\pi k / 12\) serve as bridges carrying the binary-icosahedral symmetry \(2I \cong \mathrm{SL}(2,5)\).
