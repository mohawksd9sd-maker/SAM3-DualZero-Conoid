\begin{abstract}
We present \textbf{SAM3 v4.22}, a unified non-commutative geometric model based on an infinite right conoid manifold equipped with a Dual-Zero hyperreal spectral triple and 12-bridge icosahedral symmetry. The framework derives the Einstein--Hilbert action with a geometrically fixed Newton constant, the full chiral Standard Model fermion spectrum with three generations, realistic CKM and PMNS mixing angles from overlap integrals, a stable Higgs sector, gauge boson masses, and a variational principle placing the Riemann zeta zeros on the critical line.  

Version 4.22 incorporates major advances: the full 2D Lorentzian Dirac operator with dynamical fermionic back-reaction, higher-order Dual-Zero corrections, complete 2D zero-mode asymptotics, higher Seeley--DeWitt coefficients, and a global self-consistent fit using a single scale \(\ell_0 \approx 1.052\) GeV$^{-1}$. All sectors show excellent phenomenological agreement, controlled effective-field-theory behavior, and internal mathematical consistency.
\end{abstract}

Built upon non-commutative geometry and spectral triples, the model achieves remarkable economy with only two fundamental parameters: the conoid scale \(\ell_0\) and the Dual-Zero strength \(\omega_0\).

\section{The Right Conoid Geometry}

The manifold is parametrized by
\[
x = u \cos v, \quad y = u \sin v, \quad z = \ell_0 \sin(2v),
\]

\[
ds^2 = du^2 + f(u,v)^2 \, dv^2, \qquad f(u,v) = \sqrt{u^2 + 16\ell_0^2 \cos^2(2v)}.
\]
The exact scalar curvature is
\[
R(u,v) = -\frac{32 \ell_0^2 \cos^2(2v)}{(u^2 + 16 \ell_0^2 \cos^2(2v))^2}.
\]
