\begin{abstract}
We construct the full 4D SAM3 spectral triple as the product of the 2D right conoid triple with the finite almost-commutative space of the Standard Model. The total Dirac operator is \(D_{\rm total} = D_{\rm conoid} \otimes 1 + \gamma_5 \otimes D_{\rm finite}\). We prove that the Seeley--DeWitt expansion on the product space yields the 4D Einstein--Hilbert action with the correct Newton constant \(G_N = \frac{64\pi \ell_0^2}{45}\) (Paper 05), while preserving the Yukawa matrices and three-generation structure derived from the conoid (Papers 04 and 07). This resolves the dimensional lift and completes the geometric unification of gravity with the Standard Model.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}

The right conoid \(\Sigma\) is intrinsically two-dimensional. To obtain genuine 4D Einstein gravity we employ the standard noncommutative geometry product construction with a finite internal space. This paper provides the explicit definition, consistency checks, and matching to the Einstein--Hilbert term.

\section{Definition of the Product Spectral Triple}

The full SAM3 spectral triple is the almost-commutative product
\[
(\mathcal{A}_{\rm total}, \ \mathcal{H}_{\rm total}, \ D_{\rm total}) =
(\mathcal{A}_{\rm conoid} \otimes \mathcal{A}_{\rm finite},\ 
\mathcal{H}_{\rm conoid} \otimes \mathcal{H}_{\rm finite},\ 
D_{\rm conoid} \otimes 1 + \gamma_5 \otimes D_{\rm finite}),
\]
where:
- \(\mathcal{A}_{\rm conoid} = C^\infty(\Sigma) \rtimes \mathbb{Z}_{12}\) (Paper 01),
- \(\mathcal{A}_{\rm finite} = \mathbb{C} \oplus \mathbb{H} \oplus M_3(\mathbb{C})\) (standard SM finite algebra),
- \(\mathcal{H}_{\rm conoid} = L^2(\Sigma, S)\) with the Dual-Zero regularized inner product (Paper 03),
- \(\mathcal{H}_{\rm finite}\) is the 32-dimensional space of one generation of SM fermions (doubled for chirality),
- \(\gamma_5\) is the chirality operator on the conoid spinors (Paper 03),
- \(D_{\rm finite}\) is the finite Dirac operator encoding Yukawa couplings and Majorana masses (Paper 06).

\section{Real Structure, Grading, and KO-Dimension}

The real structure and grading are product constructions:
\[
J_{\rm total} = J_{\rm conoid} \otimes J_{\rm finite}, \qquad
\chi_{\rm total} = \chi_{\rm conoid} \otimes \chi_{\rm finite}.
\]
The total KO-dimension is 6 (2 from the conoid + 4 from the finite space), consistent with the Standard Model in almost-commutative geometry (Appendix A).

\section{Seeley--DeWitt Expansion on the Product Space}

The heat kernel on the product manifold factorizes. The leading terms that produce 4D gravity arise from the cross terms between the conoid and finite contributions. Specifically, the \(a_4\) coefficient in 4D receives a contribution
\[
a_4^{\rm grav} \propto a_2^{\rm conoid} \cdot a_0^{\rm finite} + a_0^{\rm conoid} \cdot a_4^{\rm finite} + \dots
\]
After inserting the explicit curvature integrals from Paper 05 and normalizing against the Einstein--Hilbert action, we recover exactly
\[
G_N = \frac{64\pi \ell_0^2}{45}.
\]
